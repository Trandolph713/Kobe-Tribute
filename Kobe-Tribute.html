Kobe Bryant

Regarded as one of the greatest players of all time

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryants life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - The son of former NBA player Joe Bryant, he declared for the 1996 NBA draft after graduation, and was selected by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th overall pick
  • 1997 - As a rookie, Bryant earned a reputation as a high-flyer and a fan favorite by winning the 1997 Slam Dunk Contest, and he was named an All-Star by his second season.
  • 2000-2002 - Despite a feud with teammate Shaquille O'Neal, the pair led the Lakers to three consecutive NBA championships from 2000 to 2002.
  • 2006 - In 2006, he scored a career-high 81 points; the second most points scored in a single game in league history, behind Wilt Chamberlain's 100-point game in 1962
  • 2009-2010 - Bryant led the team to two consecutive championships in 2009 and 2010, and was named NBA Finals MVP on both occasions.
  • 2013 - Kobe suffered a torn Achilles tendon at age 34
  • 2015-2016 - Kobe's Injures cause his game to decline leading him to Retire.
  • 2018 - he won the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for his 2017 film Dear Basketball.
  • 2020 - Bryant died at age 41, along with his 13-year-old daughter Gianna and seven others, in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California in 2020
